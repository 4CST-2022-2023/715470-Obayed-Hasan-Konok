About Us

MISSION STATEMENT

The Student Council is dedicated to empowering the student body, advocating for student rights, and fostering a campus environment where every voice is heard. Our mission is to represent the interests of all students, provide a platform for their concerns, and work collaboratively with the administration to create positive change.

HISTORY OF
STUDENT COUNCIL

Founded in 2009, the Student Council has a long-standing tradition of advocating for student rights and enhancing campus life. Over the years, the council has led numerous initiatives, from organizing large-scale events to addressing key issues such as Qouta Protest and anti discrimination. Our council has always been driven by the belief that students are the heart of our institute, and our primary goal is to ensure their voices are central to every decision made on campus

KEY ACHIEVEMENTS

  1. Establishment of Mental Health Resources (Year): Successfully advocated for increased mental health services on campus, resulting in the creation of a dedicated counseling center.
  2. Campus Inclusivity Initiative (Year): Launched a campaign that led to the introduction of more inclusive policies and practices within the college community.
  3. Annual Charity Drive (Year): Organized an annual event that raised significant funds for local charities, showcasing the compassion and community spirit of our student body.

LOOKING AHEAD

The Student Council remains committed to its mission of student advocacy and leadership. As we look to the future, we aim to expand our initiatives, enhance student engagement, and continue to be the voice for positive change on campus.
